📊 What We Do

We aggregate live gold and silver spot prices from international financial markets including COMEX, LBMA, and FOREX exchanges, then convert them into local currencies using real-time exchange rates. Our data covers 24K, 22K, 21K, 18K, 14K and 10K gold prices per gram, tola, troy ounce, and kilogram.

🌍 Global Coverage

From the Karachi Sarafa Bazar to the Dubai Gold Souk, from Riyadh to London — goldaratetoday.org provides localised gold price data for every major precious metals market in the world. We automatically detect your location and display prices in your local currency.

📰 Expert Content

Our editorial team produces in-depth articles on the gold market, investment strategies, karat comparisons, and regional market insights. All content is original, thoroughly researched, and updated regularly to reflect current market conditions.

🔒 Data Integrity

All prices on goldaratetoday.org are sourced from reputable international financial data providers. We cross-validate prices against multiple sources to ensure accuracy.
